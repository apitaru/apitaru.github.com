Start With One

I currently lead comunity-driven innovation projects at Google Creative Lab. Our work typically starts by working together with one person to make something impactful with them and their community. If you have an idea for a project or would like to join one, don't hesitate to reach out.

Audio Visual Artist

In the early 2000's I was part of the 'Interactive Art' scene and have exhibitted widely in museums and galleries. I have recently started to publicly share work again because NFTs have activated a creative community of artists just like 20 years ago. I've missed that. I post my work regularly on Twitter (for everyone to enjoy for free) and am creating NFTs here and here for those who'd like to own one.

Teaching

I helped found the School for Poetic Computaiton (and Kitchen Table Coders) with friends after many years of teaching at NYU ITP, Cooper Union and Pratt. I have not been actively teaching for some time now but do guest lectures.
